## Description

Unlock the building blocks of the universe with the most modern, comprehensive, and interactive Periodic Table on the web, iOS, and Android platforms. Designed for students, educators, and science enthusiasts, this app transforms a static chart into a dynamic educational powerhouse. From high-school chemistry to professional research, this is the only reference tool you will ever need.

### SCIENTIFIC PRECISION AT YOUR FINGERTIPS 
Every element is detailed with high-precision data from authoritative sources. Explore 118 elements with unparalleled depth:
- Physical Properties: Atomic mass, density, melting point, and boiling point.
- Atomic Structure: Electron configurations (standard and semantic), atomic radius, covalent radius, and crystal structures.
- Chemical Behavior: Electronegativity (Pauling scale), ionization energy, electron affinity, and all common oxidation states.
- Thermodynamics: Specific heat capacity and thermal conductivity.
- Identification: Block (s, p, d, f), group and period numbers, and natural appearance descriptions.

### ELEMENT SUMMARIES
Go beyond the numbers. Our summaries provide a deep-dive biography for every single element, covering:
- Real-World Uses: From industrial manufacturing to high-tech electronics.
- History and Discovery: Learn who discovered the element, when, and the story behind its name.
- Production: How the element is extracted or synthesized today.
- Health and Safety: Critical information on biological roles and safety precautions.

### STUNNING INTERACTIVE VISUALS
- 3D Atomic Models: Rotate and explore elements in a 3D visualization space.
- Color-Coded Groups: Effortlessly identify Alkali Metals, Noble Gases, Transition Metals, Lanthanides, Actinides, and more with a curated, professional color palette.
- Intuitive Layout: Switch between standard and wide layouts optimized for everything from the smallest iPhone to the largest iPad Pro.

### THE PERFECT STUDY TOOL
- Audio Pronunciation: Master the language of chemistry with built-in element name pronunciations using the latest Web Speech technology.
- Powerful Search: Instantly find any element by name, symbol, or atomic number.
- Advanced Filtering: Filter the entire table by chemical series or category with a single tap.
- Offline Ready: Access the entire database anytime, anywhere—perfect for the lab or the classroom.
